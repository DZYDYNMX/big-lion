Expert Insights

SIGNS YOU NEED PROFESSIONAL DRAIN CLEANING

It’s difficult to gauge if you can trust a company that claims you need an entire pipe replaced when a simple clearing would do. At BigLion Plumbing, we promise you’ll only receive fair and honest feedback from our pros. What are some signs you can look for to tell when your drains need professional attention?

SLOW DRAINAGE

If water is pooling around your feet in the shower or your sinks take forever to empty, a deep blockage is forming.

FOUL ODORS

Persistent bad smells coming from your drains usually indicate rotting food or sewage trapped in the lines.

GURGLING SOUNDS

If you hear bubbling or gurgling when you flush the toilet or use the sink, it means air is trapped due to a severe clog.

FREQUENT CLOGS

If you find yourself plunging the same toilet multiple times a week, there is an underlying issue in the main line.
