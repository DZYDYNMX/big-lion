Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when requesting a quote or booking a service. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and physical property address. We use this information solely to schedule and perform plumbing services.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to dispatch technicians to your location, communicate service updates, send invoices, and maintain our internal records. We may also use your email to send occasional service reminders (e.g., annual water heater flushes), which you can opt out of at any time.

3. Information Sharing

BigLion Plumbing does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We only share information with trusted subcontractors or suppliers who are directly involved in completing your specific plumbing project, under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Privacy Policy or the handling of your data, please reach out to our team.
