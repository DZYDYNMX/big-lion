HOW TO KNOW YOU HAVE A HIDDEN LEAK

Because most of your home's plumbing is hidden behind walls and beneath concrete slabs, leaks can go unnoticed for months. Watch out for these critical warning signs that indicate you need our leak detection team immediately:

Spiking Water Bills

If your water usage hasn't changed but your bill has skyrocketed, a hidden pipe is almost certainly leaking continuously.

Warm Spots On Floors

Feeling unusual warmth beneath your feet, especially on concrete slab floors, is a strong indicator of a hot water line leak.

Mold And Mildew Smells

A persistent musty odor in certain rooms, cabinets, or basements means water is pooling in dark, unventilated spaces.

Cracks In Foundation

Slab leaks can wash away the soil supporting your home, leading to visible cracks in walls, flooring, and the foundation itself.

OUR LEAK DETECTION METHODS

Thermal Imaging

Our infrared cameras detect temperature anomalies behind walls and ceilings, finding hidden moisture without breaking any drywall.

Acoustic Pinpointing

We use ultra-sensitive listening equipment to hear pressurized water escaping underground, allowing for highly accurate localized repairs.

Slab Leak Detection

Specialized equipment tracks your water lines beneath the concrete foundation to find slab leaks before they compromise structural integrity.

Rapid Repair Solutions

Once the leak is pinpointed, we provide targeted, minimally invasive repair options to stop the damage and restore your peace of mind.
